# We Came to This World to Watch a Wonderful Show

## A Circus Arrives in a Small Town

In a novel written by a foreign author, there is a story about a circus that arrived in a small town.

Because the circus needed many helping hands, they hired temporary workers from the town.

If someone worked **three hours**, they would receive a ticket to watch the show from the outer area.

If they worked **six hours**, they could enter the inner seating area.

But if someone worked **an entire day**, they would receive a ticket to sit **in the very front row, right in the center**.

Two poor brothers had always dreamed of watching a circus performance. They wanted to see the bears, the lions, and the clowns.

However, they could not afford a ticket.

So they decided to work for the circus.

---

## A Day of Exhausting Work

The brothers began their work early in the morning.

They carried equipment, built the stage, fetched water, and swept the ground.

From sunrise to sunset, they worked tirelessly without rest.

By the end of the day, they were completely exhausted.

But their reward was worth it.

They received the **best seats in the circus**.

---

## The Best Seats… But No Energy Left

That night, the circus performance finally began.

The two boys sat in the very first row, right in the center.

Their bodies were covered with dust. Their hands and feet were full of painful blisters.

When the host appeared on stage, the audience burst into enthusiastic applause.

But the two poor boys were so exhausted that they **fell asleep in the middle of the applause**.

---

## Life Is Like a Circus Performance

The world is wonderful, just like a circus show.

Every one of us hopes to sit in the best seat and watch the performance of life.

From the moment we step into society, we constantly hear voices encouraging us to push harder, work harder, and move to the front.

But it is worth asking ourselves a question.

Would you rather do your best and happily watch the show from a comfortable seat?

Or would you rather push yourself beyond your limits and fall asleep in the best seat?

---

## When Effort Becomes Burden

Working hard and striving for success are admirable qualities.

However, we must also understand our own limits.

Many times, when we feel physically and mentally exhausted, it is because we are trying to do things beyond our abilities.

Blindly forcing ourselves forward may only push us toward unnecessary hardship.

Some people encourage others without thinking carefully.

But no matter how brave and confident a rabbit may be, it can never fly into the sky.

Blind encouragement can sometimes lead us away from the right path in life.

---

## Moral of the Story

Life is not only about reaching the best position.

True wisdom lies in finding balance between effort and enjoyment.

We came to this world not merely to struggle endlessly, but to appreciate the beauty of life.

Remember: we came here to watch a wonderful show, not to fall asleep in the best seat.
