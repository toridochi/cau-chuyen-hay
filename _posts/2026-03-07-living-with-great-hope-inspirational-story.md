# Living with Great Hope

## Alexander the Great's Greatest Treasure

Alexander the Great brought cultural exchange between Greece and the East, helping create a flourishing world that later became part of the historic Silk Road.

It is said that before launching his great expedition against Persia, Alexander distributed almost all of his wealth to his generals and soldiers.

Preparing for the long military campaign required enormous financial resources. Supplies, food, and equipment had to be purchased.

Yet Alexander gave away nearly everything he owned, from his treasured possessions to his land.

One of his generals, Perdiccas, was puzzled and asked:

“Your Majesty, if you give everything away, what will you take with you on this journey?”

Alexander replied calmly:

“My greatest treasure is **hope**.”

Hearing this answer, Perdiccas said:

“Then please allow us to share that hope with you.”

He refused the wealth that had been given to him, and many other officers followed his example.

## The Strength of Hope

Toda Josei once told young people:

“Human beings cannot live without hope. Everyone lives with hope in their hearts. If someone truly lives without hope, that person has already lost in life.”

In life, people often encounter failures and obstacles.

Some become pessimistic, lose courage, or blame others.

But those who **do not lose hope even in difficult circumstances** will eventually find a new path forward.

Deep in their hearts, they will discover the true joy of living.

---

## Hope Is the Light of Life

A life filled with hope is powerful.

A life without hope leads toward failure.

Hope is the energy that drives life forward.

Those who keep beautiful dreams in their hearts are truly fortunate.

Human beings have the unique ability to create hope for the future and shape their own destiny.

---

## The Fire of Hope

In the journey of life, the most important thing is not wealth or social status.

What truly matters is the burning flame of hope within our hearts.

Those who live for a great hope, without calculating personal gain or loss, develop courage.

They are not frightened by difficulties and can see reality with wisdom and clarity.

People who maintain hope throughout their lives possess the strongest faith and ultimately become the true winners in life.

---

## Moral of the Story

Hope is one of the greatest sources of human strength.

Even in difficult times, those who keep hope alive in their hearts can overcome obstacles, discover new paths, and create a meaningful life.
